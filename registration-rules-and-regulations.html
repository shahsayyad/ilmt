Registration Rules and Regulations

  • Each participating Runner can pitch and run only one leg out of the 65 legs.
  • Your entry and running number bib is not transferable to any other person under any circumstance Runners found doing so will be disqualified.
  • Participants are not allowed to run with babies or children who are under age.
  • Completion of the online registration form confirms the Participant’s agreement to abide by these R&R.
  • Only experienced Runners are expected to sign up for the Tiranga Relay.
  • Participants are requested to meticulously browse the I Love My Tiranga website for any additional information before signing up.
  • Confirmation of the participation of the Runners is subjected to the applicable confirmation criteria and the entry rules given herein.
