Welcome to india’s Largest Marathon For Tiranga

The first edition of I Love My Tiranga was organised & spearheaded by Mr. Khalid Qureshi in the year 2012 from Mumbai-Delhi. This year the moment takes shape of large size national marathon.

  • TO SPREAD THE LOVE FOR TIRANGA & THE NATION
  • TO INSPIRE EVERY HOME TO HAVE A TIRANGA, LIKE THEIR HOLY BOOKS

  • TO DISTRIBUTE TIRANGA & EDUCATE THE MASSES ABOUT TIRANGA

    TO VISIT MAXIMUM SCHOOLS, COLLEGES, CORPORATES, HOSPITALS, PUBLIC PLACES DURING THE RUN

  • TO CREATE AN ANNUAL MARATHON, A TRUE INDIAN, LOOKS UPTO
  • TO MAKE INDIA A BETTER PLACE TO LIVE
  • REVERENCE AND RESPECT SHALL AT ALL TIMES BE ACCORDED THE FLAG
  • WHERE THE MIND IS WITHOUT FEAR AND THE HEAD IS HELD HIGH

THE MAN BEHIND
‘I LOVE MY TIRANGA’
- KHALID QURESHI

I Love My Tiranga, an initiative by Mohd. Khalid Qureshi was executed by Tricolor Events Pvt. Ltd. in 2012. A run was organized from Gateway of India, Mumbai to India Gate, New Delhi with holding a Tiranga. Legacy to live for and legacy to die for.

Mr. Qureshi is no lesser than the living legend, what better word to describe an athlete who on his way covered a 1,900 km run with 10 halts at various cities from Mumbai to Delhi holding the Tiranga in his hand who begun his run from 26th of January 2012. The incredible attempt is to uphold, honor and love for our National Flag.

“What a wonderful year 2012 has been. A thought came in my mind that I have everything at my house except one i.e. Tiranga. This thought inspired me to take this moment forward and we should do something to induce people to keep a Tiranga at their homes. So, we actually started with the I Love My Tiranga Marathon in 2012 where we had visited 105 schools, distributed 30,000 Tirangas, influenced 60,000 students and enrolled 70 religious institutions to keep Tiranga in their premises. “– Khalid Qureshi.

In 2015, the aim is bigger and larger across the nation to promote the cause and enroll atleast 10% of the Indian Population.

Let’s do our bit to make India a better place to live. Let the world know, you are proud to be an Indian. Love your country and live with the pride. Gear up with the I Love My Tiranga’s Relay Run Marathon 26th January 2015.
